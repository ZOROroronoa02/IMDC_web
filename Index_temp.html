The Studio

We design spaces
that breathe

IMDC is a multidisciplinary architecture and interior design studio rooted in the belief that great spaces hold the power to transform daily life. We work at the intersection of structure, light, and material — crafting environments that are both rigorously functional and deeply poetic.

From private residences to cultural institutions, each project begins with careful listening — to the land, to the brief, to the people who will inhabit the space.

Our Ethos

" Teamwork makes the dream work!

We design with you, for you."

At Ipsita Mondal design co. we aim to translate the unspoken and unexpressed without you realizing. Your investment is the project and our investment is you. We believe in getting to know you, your essence, and your personality beyond the apparent to deliver a project unique to you. We ideate and iter​​ate until we hit the nail on the head. We then follow it up with dedicated execution or management to ensure our collective vision is not compromised.

What We Do

Our
expertise

Architecture →

From concept to completion — residential, commercial, cultural, and institutional buildings designed with structural rigour and spatial grace.

Interior Design →

Environments crafted from the inside out. Material, light, furniture, and detail work in concert to create spaces that feel inevitable.

03
Urban Design →

Thinking beyond the building — masterplanning, streetscapes, and public realm design that strengthen the fabric of cities.

Adaptive Reuse →

Giving old structures new life. We approach heritage buildings with care, uncovering the latent possibility in every existing wall.

Architecture does not end at the threshold. We design gardens, courtyards, and outdoor rooms that extend the life of a building into the open air.
